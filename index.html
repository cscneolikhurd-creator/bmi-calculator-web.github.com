What is BMI?

Definition

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a person's weight in kilograms divided by the square of height in meters.

Importance

BMI is a simple screening tool for weight categories that may lead to health problems.

Limitations

BMI doesn't consider muscle mass, bone density, or overall body composition.

Why Use Our BMI Calculator?

Our free BMI calculator provides instant results to help you understand your body mass index. BMI is an important indicator of overall health. Whether you're underweight, normal, overweight, or obese, our tool gives you personalized advice and ideal weight range.

How to Calculate BMI?

BMI = weight (kg) / (height (m))². For example, if you weigh 70kg and your height is 1.75m, your BMI would be 22.86 which falls in the normal weight category.

BMI Categories:

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5 to 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25 to 29.9
  • Obese: BMI 30 or greater

Remember that BMI is just one indicator of health. Always consult with a healthcare professional for personalized medical advice.
